---How to install and use Ready2Launch theme---

1,
Upload folder "ready2launch" (in same directory like this instructions) to your web (using FTP client) to folder: "-wordpress directory-/wp-content/themes/".

2,
Then head to your Wordpress administration and under Appearance select "Ready2Launch!" and activate it.

3,
After this, you should be able to access Ready2Launch! Options Page (in left sidebar under Appearance). There you can setup your theme, text, font colors and some other features.
